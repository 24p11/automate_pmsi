tf_codage.utils.grep_keras_results_from_notebook¶

tf_codage.utils.grep_keras_results_from_notebook(ipynb_file, filter_by='val_loss')[source]¶

Parse keras results from jupyter notebook.

Example

>>> df = grep_keras_results_from_notebook(
...     'tests/data/dummy_notebook.ipynb')
>>> print(df)
         loss accuracy val_loss val_accuracy
epoch

2      0.0000   0.4889   0.0000       0.4000
